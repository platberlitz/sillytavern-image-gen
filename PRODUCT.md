# Product

## Register

product

## Users

SillyTavern roleplayers, character curators, and image-generation power users who generate and manage images while a chat is active. They work in a constrained extension drawer and need both a quick everyday workflow and access to provider-specific controls without searching through a configuration dump.

## Product Purpose

Quick Image Gen makes image generation feel native to SillyTavern while supporting many remote and local providers. Success means users can understand the active prompt context, character inheritance, provider setup, and generation recipe at a glance, then generate or recover an image without knowing the extension's internal settings model.

## Brand Personality

Capable, focused, and trustworthy. QIG should retain a recognizable accent and strong generation status while using familiar SillyTavern interaction patterns everywhere else.

## Anti-references

- Schema-driven settings dumps where every field has equal visual weight.
- Deeply nested bordered cards and disclosures.
- A disconnected custom application that ignores SillyTavern's theme and controls.
- Low-contrast microcopy, decorative effects, and unfamiliar controls for standard tasks.

## Design Principles

- Organize around the generation workflow, not the settings schema.
- Show the value that will actually be sent and keep one source of truth for each setting.
- Make inherited, saved, and active character state visible.
- Preserve expert capability through progressive disclosure rather than permanent density.
- Earn trust through native behavior, clear recovery paths, and truthful labels.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for text contrast, accessible names, keyboard operation, focus management, and touch targets. Respect reduced motion, arbitrary SillyTavern themes, narrow extension drawers, mobile browser zoom behavior, and users who cannot rely on color alone.
